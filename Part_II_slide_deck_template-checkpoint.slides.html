Effect of borrower interest rate from Prosper Loan Data¶

Investigation Overview¶

Prosper is loan company, The purpose of this project is to look at the primary factors of loans and borrowers interest rate that might be used to predict their progression.

Dataset Overview¶

The Prosper loan dataset comprises of 113937 loan entries with 81 attributes on each loan which include so many attribute of borrowers eg employement status , occupation, creditgrade etc

Loan Status¶

Over 80% of all loans on Prosper are either current or paid completed. 10.5% of Prosper's loans were charged off and 4.4% all went into default. Additionally, 1.8% of debts were lumped under the classification of Past Dueand 0,2% went into finalpaymentprogress

Loan Original Amount Distribution¶

There are three peaks at 4K, 10K, and 15K, as can be seen. These three loan sizes appear to be the most popular ones that people have taken out.

Relation between Borrower Rate and Prosper Score, Prosper Rating¶

There is compelling evidence that the borrower's rate is very well predicted by the borrower's Prosper rating or score. We can infer that the rate is lower the higher the score. In a similar vein, borrowers with AA prosper ratings should anticipate an APR that will probably fall between 2-15%, while those with poor ratings (HR) can anticipate interest rates as high as 40%!

Borrower Rate by Prosper Rating and Loan terms¶

We note that the rates applied on 5-year loans are generally higher than the rates applied on other maturities, regardless of the prosper rating (with the exception of the E rating (same rates for 3 and 5 years loans ) and the HR rating)
